Flashback Events

Workshops

A workshop in colleges is a short-term, interactive session designed to teach students practical skills, enhance their knowledge, or engage them in hands-on learning experiences. Workshops can be conducted by professors, industry experts, or guest speakers and usually focus on specific topics related to academics, career development, or personal growth.

Seminars

A seminar in colleges is an academic event where experts, professors, or students present and discuss a specific topic in detail. Seminars are designed to enhance knowledge, encourage discussions, and provide insights into specialized subjects.

Cultural Fests

Cultural events in colleges are programs or activities that celebrate traditions, arts, music, dance, drama, and other creative expressions. These events provide students with a platform to showcase their talents, promote diversity, and create a vibrant campus atmosphere.

Sports Events

Sports events in colleges are competitions, tournaments, or activities that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students. These events can range from intercollege championships to friendly matches and fitness challenges.
