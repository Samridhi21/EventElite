The DAV Mantra

10 PRINCIPLES OF ARYA SAMAJ

1. God is the efficient cause of all true knowledge

2. God is existent, intelligent, and blissful.

3. The Vedas are the scriptures of all true knowledge. Aryas must read and teach them.

4. One should always be ready to accept truth and renounce untruth.

5. All acts should be performed in accordance.

6. The aim of Arya Samaj is to promote physical.

7. Our conduct should be guided by love, righteousness, and justice.

8. We should dispel ignorance (Avidya) and promote knowledge (Vidya).

9. One should seek the welfare of all rather than personal gain.

10. One should follow societal rules for welfare while maintaining personal freedom.

Our Vision

The College Emblem

Crowning the emblem is the line “Tamso Ma JyotirGamaya” which is the part of a three-line Vedic Incantation. This has been taken from the Brahadaranyaka Upanishad which forms an intrinsic part of the Yajur Veda. It is a prayer to our supreme creator to enlighten and illumine our minds thereby blessing us with the power to distinguish between right and wrong, good and evil.

Just as the Sun enables an evolution from a world of darkness to light, similarly the Light House shown in the emblem depicts how the power of education leads us from a state of avidya to vidya or from ignorance to knowledge. The burgeoning magnificence of the Light House, the stellar brightness of the rays reaching afar further underscores the transforming potential of quality education.

Our College endeavors to strike a perfect poise between antiquity and contemporaneity, convention, and modernity. Our enterprise is to create an amalgam of tradition and technology. We strive to disseminate education in consonance with upcoming trends, global challenges, and topical necessities never losing sight of truth, correct knowledge, and righteousness. Education for us is a never-ending process of reaching out, illuminating the deepest niche in the minds of the youth of today. Like the magnificent rays of Light, education for us symbolizes an undying quest for knowledge towards the pursuit of excellence.
